Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wondered about the history of the chair? Well now you can learn more! Through this playful website you can learn everything that you didn't know you needed about the extensive history of our seating stabilizers! You will be able to share with your friends tens of useful facts about chairs that will make the beg you to stop! You will be the expert on chairs no matter where you go!

Audience

This site is perfect for those searching for a useless skill to turn into a useful skill! If you want to annoy the crap out of your friends and boast that you are the most intelligent person in this room then keep scrolling because this is the site for you!
