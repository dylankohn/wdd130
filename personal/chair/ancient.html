The ancient history of the chair has interesting designs with artistic touches laced in the back and seat of the chair. These chairs were not only made for their practical use but for decoration as well.

The chair was a great invention at the time as it allowed people to relax and be comfortable when they were eating, working, or doing any other job. This allowed people to be happier and have a better way of life.

As described previously the ancient chair started off with a very basic shape. It got the job done with minimal comfort and the bare minimum of functionality. It was a cheap chair that the common folk could easily afford.

This chair was made for the upper class or kings of the time. It was a very ornate chair with a lot of designs and intracacies ingrained in it and was very beautiful drawing the peoples eyes to whoever was sitting upon it. These chairs often had designs on them that told stories of the people and the rise of the person who sat upon it.
