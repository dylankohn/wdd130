The History of the Chair

Home Ancient Renaissance

The chair has had its place in society for a long time. It has been a tool of comfort for thousands of years and has had its share of designs and modifications over the years. Over time it transitioned from a simple elevated platform to the chair as we know it today.

In this website we will take you through the history of the chair and its many different designs through the centuries. We will explore the different cultures that contributed and what certain designs meant in the meaning and importance of the chair.

The first chair started out as a simple short chair with a straight back flat seat and no arm rests. This was as simple as it gets in shape and served well for resting the legs of those who sat in it.

Now we have more modern chairs that we are more familiar with. This chair has four longer legs that allows for the more comfortable position of the feet. It also has a more bowl like shape and a padded seat to be the most comfortable. There are no visible arm rests however the sides of the chair are at the correct level to comfortably rests ones arms on the chair.
