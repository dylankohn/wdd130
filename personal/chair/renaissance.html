Renaissance History

Home Ancient Renaissance

As the renaissance came the style of chair became much more elegant and comfortable. Comfort was starting to be implemented more as well as intricate designs and patterns.

Different styles of chair started becoming more popular and people started seeing chairs as a sign of wealth and luxury. The more intricate and fancy the more respected and wealthy you were.

The lounge chair started to pick up in popularity for its comfort and price. It was often the symbol of status and elegant parties and balls and was often reserved for the guest of honor.

The dining chair also increased in comfort and design. Fancier and fancier designs started to appear and they would be reserved for the most elegant and fancy dinner parties. If every a person of power or wealth came for supper this would be the dining set to impress them.
