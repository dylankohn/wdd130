What to Expect

On this fun filled adventure you can find roaring rapids, small waterfalls, and lots of fun! There are lazy river sections where you can float down along side the boat! You can take a nice picnic at the lunch spot to get to know your date! Don't forget about the romantic waterfall at the end of the day to take a nice picture and maybe share a romantic spark!

Location

This river sits in the bottom of a valley that stretches high into the air! Find breathtaking views and lots of wildlife on the shoreline and find many lagoons and waterfalls along the way! Perfect location for that first date with that special someone! High in the mountains it pays homage to that typical adventure movie and expresses beauty in the best of ways!
