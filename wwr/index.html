About the River

On the river you will find terrain suited to every skill level. You can find nice easy eddies and lulls, or you can find rapids that will satisfy the adrenaline junky inside you. The river also provides places to swim and go down rapids outside of the boat! At lunch there will be a riverbank to have a nice picnic and get to know your date! Everything on you adventure is tailored to be able to get to know your date better. This allows for the possibility to get to know someone and lead to a relationship in the future!
